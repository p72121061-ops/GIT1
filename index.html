🪳 What is RoachWatch?
RoachWatch is an independent, non-partisan accountability platform tracking government promises, exam paper leaks, and India's global standing — with verified sources on every claim.

The name is inspired by the Cockroach Janta Party (CJP) movement of 2026, when India's Chief Justice called unemployed youth "cockroaches." The youth didn't protest — they built. So did we.
📋 How We Classify Promises
❌ Broken — Promise made, clearly not delivered. Data from government or independent sources confirms failure.

💀 Done Badly — Promise technically fulfilled but implementation was so poor it caused harm, or the outcome was the opposite of intent.

⏳ Pending — Promise made, deadline passed or approaching, status unclear or incomplete.

✅ Kept — Promise made and delivered, verified by independent sources.
🔍 Our Source Standards
Every card on this platform links to at least one of the following:

GOV — Government Documents COURT — SC / HC Orders REPORT — Research Reports NEWS — Established Publications ACADEMIC — Peer Reviewed

We do not publish claims without at least one verifiable source. If a source link is missing, that card is marked for review.
